What we offer

A Real Connection with Real People

A Social Network for Migrant Workers

Moving and adjusting to a new home is difficult. Our goal is to make it easier for you! Different places have vastly different ways of life. Wouldn't it be great if you already had friends where you are planning to move? We are here to make that possible! 

A Social Network for Travelers

Traveling is one of the best joys of life! It becomes even more exciting in the company of friends. We live in an increasingly connected world, yet our world is more complex than ever. We want you to travel with the peace and comfort of knowing that a friend is nearby, when needed. 

A Social Network for 'Global Locals'

Planet Earth is one big family. The internet is great (we love it!), but it does not replace "insider" tips and advice from well-informed locals. We cordially invite you to join us as a "Global Local' who help expatriates, migrant workers, tourists, and travelers become familiar with their new surroundings!

Reduce uncertainty on your next journey.
